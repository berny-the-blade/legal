Privacy Policy — Domino Pernambucano

Last updated: May 11, 2026
Developer: Tablebound Studios
Contact: tableboundstudios@gmail.com

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Domino Pernambucano mobile application (the "App"), developed by Tablebound Studios ("Developer", "we"), collects, uses, and protects user information ("you"). By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

This policy is compliant with:

  • LGPD — Brazilian General Data Protection Law (Law 13.709/2018)
  • GDPR — EU General Data Protection Regulation
  • CCPA — California Consumer Privacy Act
  • Google Play Data Safety policy

2. Data we collect

2.1 Data you provide directly

  • Player nickname / profile — chosen by you (e.g., "John", "Maria") inside the App. We do not require real names, email, phone, or date of birth.

2.2 Data collected automatically

  • Gameplay telemetry — for each round we record:
    • Move sequence (tiles played, side, passes)
    • Round outcomes (score, win type — batida, carroça, lá e ló, cruzada, blocked)
    • Decision time per move
    • Anonymous device identifier
    • Timestamp
  • Android Advertising ID — used by advertising partners (see Section 5).

2.3 Data we do NOT collect

  • Real name, surname, address, government ID, or other identity documents
  • Email address (unless you contact us voluntarily)
  • Phone number
  • Precise location (GPS)
  • Contents of other apps on your device
  • Contact list, messages, photos

3. How we use your data

  • Gameplay telemetry — to improve the App's artificial intelligence, identify bugs, and develop future versions. Data is aggregated and anonymized and may be used to train machine-learning models.
  • In-app purchases — processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not store any credit card or payment data.
  • Advertisements — served by Google AdMob (see Section 5).
  • Customer support — if you contact us, we use your email address only to respond.

We do not sell or rent your data to third parties for marketing.

4. Legal basis for processing (LGPD / GDPR)

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance (LGPD Art. 7º V; GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)) — to provide the game
  • Legitimate interest (LGPD Art. 7º IX; GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)) — for product improvement and aggregate analytics
  • Consent (LGPD Art. 7º I; GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)) — for personalized advertising (revokable in Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset Advertising ID / Opt out)

6. In-app purchases

The App offers a single one-time purchase "Remove Ads" (price approximately $3.99 USD / R$ 19.99 BRL or equivalent). This purchase:

  • Is processed entirely by Google Play Billing
  • Is linked to your Google Play account, not your device
  • Can be restored on other devices using the same account
  • Does not involve card data being stored by the Developer

For refunds: contact Google Play Support at support.google.com/googleplay.

7. Data retention

  • Gameplay telemetry: retained for up to 24 months for analytics and AI training. After this period, records are permanently anonymized or deleted.
  • Purchase records: minimal records required by tax law may be retained up to 5 years.

8. Your rights (LGPD / GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access — know what data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion / Right to be forgotten — request deletion of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in structured format
  • Opt-out / Object — object to processing (especially targeted ads)
  • Withdraw consent — at any time

To exercise these rights, email tableboundstudios@gmail.com with the subject "LGPD", "GDPR", or "CCPA". We respond within 15 business days.

9. Children's privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If a guardian identifies that a child has provided data, contact us and we will remove it immediately.

10. Security

We employ reasonable technical measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS / TLS connections
  • Backend (Firebase) access restricted by authentication
  • Storage on Google Cloud servers with encryption at rest

No system is 100% secure. In case of a data breach, we will notify the ANPD (Brazilian Data Protection Authority) and affected users as required by LGPD.

11. International transfers

Data may be stored on Google servers outside Brazil (primarily US and EU). These transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission and equivalent mechanisms under LGPD.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy. Significant changes will be communicated in the App. The latest version is always available at this URL.
